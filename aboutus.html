About us


  • Wellness Hospital is a 200 bedded Multispecialty Hospital located in Coimbatore, providing 24×7 services. This hospital was started with a vision to bridge the unmet need in healthcare. Our strength is in providing uninterrupted patient care at an affordable cost with the sole betterment of health. Our motto is to advance health care at an affordable cost all under one roof. In your most stressful moments, we want to be the most supportive. We consider the physical as well as the emotional quotient of any that makes the overall experience at WH better.
  • Health is our most valuable wealth. Healthcare is one of the fundamental needs in our lives. Health care has evolved leaps and bounds in the past century and is continuing to do so at a fast pace. Keeping this in mind, my vision was to start a facility where advanced healthcare is available at an affordable cost. Over the years we could see that in order to provide a more wholesome patient-centered care, we need to upgrade to a facility where most amenities are available under one roof. It gives me immense joy and satisfaction that I was able to realize my vision.
  • There is a palpable sense of disillusion currently among many about the healthcare system as a whole. In Wellness we will do our best to make sure that anyone entering our facility feels at ease here. Every member who is a part of the Wellness Hospital family is committed to the one thing that matters most “your health”. For many more years to come, we intend to lend our support to people from all walks of life.








VISION
