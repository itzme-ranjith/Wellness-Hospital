Cardiology

The Department of Cardiology at Wellness is one of the finest in any private institution in the state. We have decades of experience dealing with every eventuality and complication resulting from myocardial infarction, multivessel disease, chronic total occlusion, and so on.

Neurology

Neurology Department is guided by internationally accepted protocol and integrated care pathways. The busy department has 3 consultants and treats over 200 outpatients and 50 inpatients every day. It also has an epilepsy surgery programme and high quality neurophysiology lab.

Psychiatry

Our department of psychiatry excels at the compassionate treatment of those afflicted by mental illness. While ‘psychotic’ is a commonly used word, in medical parlance it describes a specific condition.

Pediatrics

We provide the very best care for children suffering from a host of conditions. Our specialist connects well with your child, We recognize the need for both holistic as well as highly specialized care in order for our little children to thrive and we take great pride in providing the best of it.

Ophthalmology

The Ophthalmology Department at Wellness Hospital aims to provide the best eye care treatment. Despite the presence of the best eye care hospitals, Wellness’s aim with the department of Opthalmology is a full-fledged Eye Hospital that prevents blindness and restores maximum vision to the patient.
